About

Throughout the program students will learn about the industry of metalworking as well as the proper saftey that is used when welding. This program also teaches students valuable life skills that can be utilized far outside the world of welding.

West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.

The West-MEC welding program is available for High School students in their Junior and/or Senior year. You can learn more about the West-MEC welding program by navigating around this site and if you have any questions do not hesitate to check out the contact page. If you are interested in the program please click the apply button below.



Throughout the first year in the West-Mec welding program the students will go in depth on the industry standards for welding practices. This includes learning the proper saftey when working, all the way to learning how to prepare the proper construction diagrams. Students will also have a hands on expirence when it comes to welding. Students will also have the opportunity to participate in the Skills USA competition, where students can show off their skills to recieve awards at the state and national levels.

Following the first year the students will strengthen their knowledge in the welding field and learn more about the proper way to plan and execute a project. They will also go in more depth about how to handle them selves when using welding tools.
